pub fn colored_diff(before: &str, after: &str) -> String
Expand description

Computes a line-based diff between before and after strings, returning a string that includes all lines, marking changed lines with - or +, and printing git-style hunk headers.

Unchanged lines are prefixed with a space. Changed lines from before are prefixed with - (in red). Changed lines from after are prefixed with + (in green).
